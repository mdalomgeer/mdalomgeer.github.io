## Group

### Masters Students

**Rajmi Doshi**

Worked on: A Patient-centered Approach to Evaluating Large Language Model-generated Responses to Patients' Questions. (Under Review-JMIR)

• Conducted a study to evaluate Large Language Models (LLMs) on their ability to provide patient-centered responses for laboratory test questions, focusing on readability, completeness, personalization, transparency, and emotional support.

• Comparing LLM responses to those from an online health community to assess alignment with patient support needs.

**Rushali Sreedhar, Sanika Bishnoi, Yachi Jitendrabhai Patel**

Working on: Building a Framework and Toolkit for Effective Participatory AI Engagement in Hard-to-Reach Populations. Funded by: Google Society-Centered AI Grant

• Leading a Google-funded project to build a participatory AI framework that empowers underserved patients in low-income areas to understand and use their medical records.

• Collaborating with Baltimore community health organizations to enhance health literacy and address healthcare access disparities.
