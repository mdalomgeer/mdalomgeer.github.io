My name is Md Alomgeer Hussein. I am a PhD student in the Information Systems department at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County. My research focuses on natural language processing and human computer interaction, with an emphasis on health-related applications. I study how people interact with AI systems in sensitive, high-stakes contexts such as healthcare, and how language-driven systems shape understanding, trust, and decision making. I earned my BSc and MSc in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the University of Dhaka, Bangladesh.
